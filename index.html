About

Alok is User Experience Designer with around 10 years of experience working with global clients helping them with best in class user experience for their products and services. He is specialist designer with focus in user centric approach of problem solving. He helps clients with their product design strategy, conducting user research, ideate & validate, and to measure success.

During his tenure, he has worked with leading brand in banking, pharmaceutical, automobile and more, delivering MVP to mid and large size projects. He has led many successful projects where he helped client setting up design system with standard practice, guidelines, and reusable assets. He facilitates design workshops, coordinates with stakeholder to deliver business and user expectation in agile setup.

In his current position at HCL, he is responsible for Design strategy and decisions for many mid and large size projects. Currently aligned with largest toy making brand, delivered award winning intranet with enhanced employee service experience. And now helping customer with digital solutions for their various business needs.

Apart from regular job, he passionately follows design world and is active member of many design community.
