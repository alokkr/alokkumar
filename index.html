More about me

Let me start from the begining. Fascinated by the creative world, I did my graduation in Animation & Multimedia design. Slowly this transitioned into an interactive digital world where I started my career as a design trainee. The growing interest in interaction design and human psychology has transformed me into the UX designer I am today.

Recent accomplishments:

  • Contributed designing a toolkit that was showcased in the COP26 event in Glasgow and received much appreciation. This toolkit recommends solutions for cities to reduce carbon footprint based on the city measures of population, growth rate, etc.

  • Prior to this, I contributed to designing an intranet that was awarded best intranet in the IntraTeam event 2020.

Apart from my regular job, I passionately follow the design world, mentor young designers, and participate in design communities. I read books (trying to) realted to business, cognitive psychology, self-help. Recent learning includes intersection of business and design (a fine line -by Hartmut Esslinger).

And, I enjoy exploring new places and meeting new people.
